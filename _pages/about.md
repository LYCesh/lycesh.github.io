I am a PhD student at [Arizona State University](https://www.asu.edu/), working in cryptography with a focus on **secure multi-party computation (MPC)** — protocols that let mutually distrusting parties jointly compute over their private inputs without revealing them.

This site is a work in progress. Publications, projects, and notes will appear here as the work develops. The best way to reach me is by email — see the icons below.
